What Is Full Truck Load (FTL)?

FTL means your cargo occupies an entire truck — no sharing, no consolidation stops. The truck is loaded at your facility and travels directly to the destination. This gives you maximum control over timing, security, and transit speed.

For manufacturers, exporters, and industrial companies moving large volumes regularly, FTL is the most cost-effective and reliable solution. Express Goods Carrier operates FTL services across all 28+ Indian states, with vehicles ranging from 9-ton LCVs to 25-ton heavy trucks and multi-axle Volvo rigs.

Who Is FTL Best Suited For?

FTL is ideal when you're moving goods that fill a truck, when cargo is time-critical and can't afford consolidation delays, or when your goods need dedicated handling without exposure to other shipments. Manufacturers, chemical companies, aluminium processors, steel distributors, and FMCG companies are our most common FTL customers.
